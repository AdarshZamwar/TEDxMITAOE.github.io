Dr. Shabbir Indorewala
Dr. Shabbir is a veteran Medical Practitioner, an Otolaryngologist, and ENT connoisseur. He is an academician with degrees of MS ENT, DORL, MB BOM. The talk will be based on the new method of surgery that he pioneered, dubbed the ‘Stitchless Surgery’.
Harsh & Prachi
Harsh and Prachi are full-time travel content creators & digital marketing entrepreneurs. They embarked on their journey to explore the world in January 2021, and within 10 months they have already covered 2 countries & 14 Indian states so far. They live by their motto ‘Go after your dreams, no matter how big, and seize each day’s potential to the fullest’.
Abhishek kar
Abhishek Kar is an investor, entrepreneur, trader, and consultant. He is also an authour, podcaster, and YouTuber. He frequently writes for business magazines. He is one of the most followed writers globally on the subject of financial markets and stock markets on Quora. Imbibed with a firm belief that 'merely existing is not living', he has been working hard to prove that nothing is impossible.
